# Applepie
## Ingredients
* Apples
* Butter
* Light Brown Sugar
* Granulated Sugar
* Nutmeg
* Lemon
* Flour
* Cinnamon

# Instructions
* In a large bowl, combine the sliced apples, granulated sugar, light brown sugar, flour, cinnamon, nutmeg, and lemon juice and lemon zest; toss to coat evenly.
